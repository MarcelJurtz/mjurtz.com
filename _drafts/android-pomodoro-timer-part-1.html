In this series, I'll be building a pomodoro timer for android. I use the pomodoro technique to increase my productivity and avoid distractions. Basically, the concept is to focus on a topic for 25 minutes, then have a break for 5 minutes. After repeating this four times, you'll get a longer break of 15 minutes.
